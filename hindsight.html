overview

Hindsight Investments is a startup looking to build a platform to keep track of your stock purchases, share and discover ideas in a community, and build your own profile. Investing in stocks is an intimidating task and other stock investing platforms tend to also reflect that complexity, thereby excluding the retail market. Hindsight aims at making investing in stocks a user friendly process by making an approachable and modern platform that everyone can use.

What I did:

  • Designed UI mockups in Photoshop.
  • Used minimalistic design to create an approachable UI.
  • Completely redesigned a Bootstrap prototype to an AngularJS SPA.
  • Developed various UI components including login/register module, dashboard, and community circle.
  • Worked with back-end intern to connect front-end and back-end functionalities.
  • Created landing page to promotoe platform.
